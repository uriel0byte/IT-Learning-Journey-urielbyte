# Description
Can you convert the number 42 (base 10) to binary (base 2)?

# Hints
1. Submit your answer in our competition's flag format. For example, if your answer was '11111', you would submit 'picoCTF{11111}' as the flag.

# Steps
1. Fire up CyberChef.
2. Select From Base, and To Base to Recipe section.
3. Change the Radix of From Base to 10 and the Radix of To Base to 2 (Binary). Insert the input field with 42.
4. Form the correct flag form.

Answer: picoCTF{101010}
